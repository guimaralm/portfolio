About Me

Hello there! My name is Guillermo Martínez, UX/ Product Designer, Graduate in Law, Master in Advocate Practice and Postgraduate in Compliance.

After four years working as corporate lawyer, I have decided to change my career direction to a field related to two of my passions, new technologies and people: UX design.

As UX designer I strive for understanding the people's needs and problems and connect them to the businesses' goals through design solutions that really matter.
